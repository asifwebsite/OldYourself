    1950 HAND MADE WOODEN BOX

    Item details

    It is handcrafted and painted by a Bulgarian master – artisan. The angles are formed by the dovetailed technique, without nails. The box is very special because it is typical for the region of the Balkan Peninsula. They are produced by artisans and every object has a unique drawing. In the past, men used these boxes to store their shaving accessories. At the front line, soldiers used to store their personal documents and family photographs in such boxes. Ladies used these boxes to store their jewelry. The measures are: 19 x 9.5 x 5 cm. / 7.5 x 3.5 x 2”/, deep 36 mm. /1.4”/.
