    Item details

    This rare enamelled GREEN metal-bodied antique telephone was made by "BELL TELEPHONES" in Antwerp, Belgium in the 1950s. On both sides of the body it has "Bell Telephones Antwerp" inscribed in gold. It also has a carrying handle. It is fitted with a beautiful original-looking plaited cord which blends beautifully with the body. This telephone is in superb condition for its age and is ready to use on a normal telephone line worldwide. The phone has a wonderful old ring & is heavy around 2.5 kgs. The heavy black handset is made of "BAKELITE" a material which was replaced by plastic in the late 1950's. Dimensions in inches : Height 4.5", Depth 6", Base 6.5" , Handset Width 9".
